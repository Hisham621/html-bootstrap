RAJASTHAN

THE DESERT STATE OF INDIA

JAIPUR

Jaipur, also known as the Pink City, is famous for its stunning palaces, forts, and vibrant culture. Key attractions include the Amber Fort, City Palace, and Hawa Mahal. The city blends royal history with modern charm, making it a top destination in Rajasthan.

AJMEER

Ajmer, a historic city in Rajasthan, is known for its spiritual significance, especially the revered Ajmer Sharif Dargah. The city also features beautiful architecture like the Taragarh Fort and Ana Sagar Lake. Ajmer is a cultural hub, blending Islamic and Rajput influences.

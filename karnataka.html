CHICKMANGLUR

Chikmagalur is a picturesque hill station located in the Western Ghats of Karnataka. Known for its coffee plantations, it is often referred to as the "Coffee Land of Karnataka." The region offers lush green landscapes, serene lakes, and numerous trekking trails. It is home to popular tourist spots like Mullayanagiri, the highest peak in Karnataka, and the Baba Budangiri hills. Chikmagalur is a peaceful destination, ideal for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike.

MYSORE PALACE

Mysore Palace, located in Karnataka, is a stunning example of Indo-Saracenic architecture. It is the official residence of the Wadiyar dynasty and a popular tourist attraction. The palace is known for its magnificent design, with intricate carvings, paintings, and grand courtyards. It is especially famous for its beautiful lighting during the annual Dussehra festival..
